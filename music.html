Albums

Released on December 25th, 2023, "When the Sun Burns Red, Think of Me" was recorded as a Christmas gift for my father. It includes several piano pieces that I wrote during undergrad, and I wanted to share them with him in a way that would be easy to access. Each song was written for a specific person who had a significant impact on my life.



Singles

As I was learning to animate, I decided to make a video for one of my songs. "Tranquil Haze" was my first attempt at this, and it took an entire day to render on my computer! This may be one of my favorites I've rendered to date, and I hope to create trippier animations in the future.





"What I Hope To See" was my second attempt at animating a video for my music, and it took even longer to render. I was still new to Blender and did a horrible job optimizing the rendering process. The final animation took many days and warmed up my bedroom in the process. Now, I make sure to run my renders while I am out of the house...
